ABOUT IEEE WIE

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the world’s largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, inspiring them to excel in their fields, and fostering leadership and innovation.

What is IEEE WIE?

What is IEEE CUET WIE Affinity Group Student Branch?

It is a student branch within the IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET). It specifically focuses on the Women in Engineering (WIE) affinity group, aiming to support and advance women in technical fields. Being established in October 2018, it serves as a platform for female students to engage in technical activities, leadership development, and career advancement.

Vision

  • • To increase the involvement of young females in technical fields.
  • • To empower women engineers and support their success in their careers.
  • • To advance technology for the beneficial activities of humanity.

Mission

  • • To provide motivation and learning opportunities for female students in engineering.
  • • To create a supportive platform for young enthusiasts with diverse talents to innovate.
  • • To organize workshops and seminars focusing on engaging women in technological fields.
  • • To organize large national technical events such as techspark.

Why Join IEEE CUET WIE Affinity Group Student Branch?

Career Development

Gain valuable skills and knowledge to enhance your career prospects in engineering and technology.

Networking

Connect with fellow female students, professionals, and industry experts.

Leadership Opportunities

Develop leadership skills through organizing events and taking on leadership roles within the branch.

Technical Advancement

Participate in workshops, seminars, and technical events like Techspark to expand your technical knowledge.

Community Support

Be part of a supportive community that encourages and empowers women in engineering.

Innovation

Contribute to innovative projects and initiatives that advance technology for the benefit of humanity.

Personal Growth

Gain confidence and develop your personal and professional skills.
