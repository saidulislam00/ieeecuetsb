About IEEE RAS CUET SBC

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society focuses on state‑of‑the‑art robotics research, automation technologies, and hands‑on engineering practices.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower students in robotics, automation, AI systems, and multidisciplinary innovation through workshops, competitions, and collaborative engineering projects.

Our Vision

To develop globally competitive engineers and researchers capable of shaping the future of intelligent machines and autonomous systems.
