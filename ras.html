About IEEE RAS CUET SBC

IEEE Robotics and Automation Society CUET Chapter focuses on promoting learning in robotics, automation, AI and related areas. It organizes workshops, events and outreach activities to help students understand and work with these technologies. The chapter aims to create a space where students can learn, practice and collaborate.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is reflected through the following core goals:
- Inspiring students to learn robotics, AI, and automation through practical activities.
- Building a community that supports curiosity, idea-sharing and collaboration.
- Encouraging students to apply their skills to create useful and meaningful robotics solutions.

Our Vision

Our vision is guided by the following goals:
- Empowering students to grow into capable contributors in robotics and intelligent technologies.
- Encouraging innovative thinking to address real-world problems through automation and AI.
- Promoting a research-oriented mindset that drives exploration and technical progress.
- Strengthening the link between technology and society through collaborative learning and outreach.
