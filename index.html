Outstanding Student Branch Award

IEEE Bangladesh Section

Honored as the Outstanding Student Branch among 61 branches across Bangladesh, recognizing our exceptional technical leadership, innovative projects, and dedicated community engagement throughout the year. Presented by Prof. M. Imamul Hassan Bhuiyan (IEEE Bangladesh Section Chair), M. Tanseer Ali (Secretary), Engr. Sohel Rana (Treasurer), and Shahriar Khan (Vice Chair, Technical) on 30 December 2025 at The Green Lounge, Dhaka.

  • Date: 30 December 2025
  • Venue: The Green Lounge, Dhaka
  • Ranking: #1 among 61 branches in Bangladesh
