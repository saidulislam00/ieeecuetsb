About IEEE PES CUET SBC

The IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) is the world's largest forum for sharing the latest in technological developments in the electric power industry, focusing on sustainable energy, grid modernization, and power electronics.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the leading provider of scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and the preferred professional development source for CUET students.

Our Vision

To enable a sustainable future powered by clean, efficient, and reliable energy, fostering a community of engineers capable of solving the global energy crisis.
