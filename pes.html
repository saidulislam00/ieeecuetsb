About IEEE PES CUET SBC

IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) CUET Student Branch Chapter has recently commenced its journey, with a commitment to advancing knowledge and fostering professional development in the field of power and energy engineering. Although in its nascent stage, the chapter endeavors to promote research, innovation, and collaborative initiatives and it aspires to make significant contributions to both the academic and professional landscape in the years ahead.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the leading provider of scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and the preferred professional development source for CUET students.

Our Vision

To enable a sustainable future powered by clean, efficient, and reliable energy, fostering a community of engineers capable of solving the global energy crisis.
