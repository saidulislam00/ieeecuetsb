About IEEE APS CUET SBC

IEEE APS focuses on electromagnetic science, antenna design, wave propagation, RF systems, and advanced wireless communication research.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To inspire students in RF engineering, electromagnetics, antenna modeling, microwave circuits, and advanced propagation systems.

Our Vision

To cultivate the next generation of antenna designers and propagation researchers, driving innovation in 5G, satellite systems, and wireless technologies.
