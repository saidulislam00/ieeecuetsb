About IEEE EMBS CUET SBC

The IEEE EMBS focuses on applying engineering concepts and methods to biological and medical systems, covering everything from medical devices and instrumentation to bioinformatics and healthcare robotics.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To equip students with interdisciplinary knowledge in biology, medicine, and engineering, enabling the development of next-generation biomedical sensors, diagnostic tools, and therapeutic devices.

Our Vision

To establish CUET as a hub for biomedical innovation, producing engineers capable of solving complex health challenges through neuroengineering, medical imaging, and computational biology.

Bioinformatics Horizon

The workshop “Bioinformatics Horizon: From Basics to Breakthroughs” has officially kicked off, bringing an exciting start to our journey into bioinformatics. Organized by IEEE EMBS CUET Student Chapter with support from the CUET Biomedical Engineering Society, the first session introduced participants to the basics and future scope of the field.
