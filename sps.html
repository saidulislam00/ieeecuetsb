About IEEE SPS CUET SBC

The IEEE Signal Processing Society focuses on the theory and application of processing signals representing information, including sound, images, sensor data, and communication protocols.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a platform for students to master the skills needed for digital signal processing (DSP), machine learning algorithms, and real-time audio/image analysis.

Our Vision

To develop a community of skilled engineers capable of designing innovative solutions in communications, biomedical imaging, and autonomous systems using advanced signal processing techniques.
