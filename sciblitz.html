11 — Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

SciBlitz 2.0 is open nationwide. University students can enter all four segments. Class 9–12 students can participate in the Project Showcase and STEM BEE. STEM BEE is open to all age groups. The AI Challenge is open to all enrolled students at any university, college, or polytechnic institute in Bangladesh.

The initial Round 1 registration fee is ৳300. If your team qualifies for the Final Round (top 60 teams), an additional fee of ৳900 will be required for confirmation. You only pay the second fee if you advance — no upfront payment for the final round.

The 3MT Final Round is a live on-stage presentation at CUET on July 10 & 11, 2026. You have exactly 3 minutes to present your research using a single static slide. There is no Q&A session. All 60 qualified finalists must be physically present at the venue.

Yes! You can register for multiple segments. Each segment has its own registration fee and form. Make sure to submit separate registrations for each event you wish to join. Note that for the AI Challenge, a participant may only be a member of one team.

Teams must submit: (1) a live public URL for their deployed product, (2) a project report (PDF, max 8 pages), (3) a public GitHub repository with commit history during the competition window, (4) a 3–5 minute demo video on YouTube or Google Drive, and (5) a Model & Data Card. See the full rulebook for details.

The final event will be held on-campus at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), Chittagong, Bangladesh on July 10 & 11, 2026.
