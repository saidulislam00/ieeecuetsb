03 — Flagship Event

3 Minute Thesis — Round Structure

A rigorous three-stage journey from abstract submission to live presentation at CUET campus.

Round 1
Abstract & Flowchart

Submit your research abstract along with a structured work flowchart. Deadline: June 7. Results: June 16.

Round 2
Video Presentation

Record and submit a 3-minute video presentation of your research. Deadline: June 24. Results: July 2.

Round 3 — Final
On-Stage at CUET

Finalists present live before a panel of expert judges on campus. July 10 & 11.

06 — Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

SciBlitz 2.0 is open nationwide. University students can enter all four segments. Class 9–12 students can participate in the Project Showcase and STEM BEE. STEM BEE is open to all age groups.

Yes! You can register for multiple segments. Each segment has its own registration fee and form. Make sure to submit separate registrations for each event you wish to join.

The initial Round 1 registration fee is ৳300. If your team qualifies for Round 2, an additional fee of ৳900 BDT will be required. You only pay the second fee if you advance — no advance payment needed for Round 2.

The video must be exactly 3 minutes long. It should present your full research clearly, with a single static slide allowed as a visual aid. Specific technical requirements and submission guidelines will be shared with qualified teams after Round 1 results.

The final event will be held on-campus at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), Chittagong, Bangladesh on July 10 & 11, 2025.

AI Hackathon details including timeline, problem statement format, and specific rules are currently being finalized. Follow our official channels for updates — an announcement will be made well before the registration deadline.

Payment instructions will be provided within the registration form. Accepted methods and transaction details will be specified per segment. Keep your transaction ID for confirmation.
