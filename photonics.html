Illuminate Your Future

About IEEE PHO CUET SBC

The IEEE Photonics Society is dedicated to advancing the field of light science and its applications, focusing on lasers, fiber optic communications, optical sensing, and photonic integrated circuits.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower students in the design, fabrication, and application of photonic devices, fostering innovation that drives the next generation of high-speed data transfer and optical technologies.

Our Vision

To be the primary platform for CUET students to engage with cutting-edge research in photonics, developing experts who will shape the future of light-speed communication and bio-sensing.

Basic PHOTONICS

The IEEE Photonics Society CUET Chapter organized an engaging “Basic PHOTONICS” workshop that introduced students to the fundamentals of photonics and its emerging research frontiers. Led by Dr. Md. Istiaque Reja, the session blended core concepts, global research insights, and practical guidance on thesis development and scientific publication.

With 70+ participants, the workshop fostered curiosity, mentorship, and multidisciplinary collaboration—motivating many undergraduates to begin their own photonics-focused research journeys.

Photonics for Emerging Applications in Communication and Imaging

IEEE CUET Student Branch hosted a session on the growing role of photonics in communication and imaging technologies. Dr. Kazi Tanvir Ahmed shared key insights on photonic systems, integrated devices, and current research trends, followed by an interactive Q&A with CUET students.
